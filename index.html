Three Steps
One Way to be Saved

"That if you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved."

Romans 10:9-10

A

Acknowledge

Acknowledge that you are a sinner. And only by the blood of Jesus that your sins can be forgiven and will cleanse you from all unrighteousness.

B

Believe

Believe in the Lord Jesus. That He was sent to die on the cross for the forgiveness of our sins.

C

Confess

Confess with your mouth that Jesus is your Lord and believe in your heart that He was raised from the dead.

Receiving Jesus as your Lord and Savior and putting your faith in Him brings The gift of Eternal life. For by grace we are saved through faith not by works so that no man will boast.

"For by grace you have been saved through faith, and this is not your own doing; it is the gift of God."

Ephesians 2:8-9

Acceptance Prayer

Dear Heavenly Father,

I confess that I am a sinner and acknowledge that I cannot save myself.

Thank you for sending your Son Jesus to die on the cross for the forgiveness of my sin. With His blood my sins are washed and I am forgiven.

Now, I confess with my mouth that Jesus is my Lord and my Savior. And believe in my heart that Jesus was raised from the dead.

With this I am saved and I received the gift of eternal life. I believe my name has now been written in the book of life and become Your child.

This I pray in Jesus Name,

Amen.
